Our story

“

As a chairman for a local soccer club, and soccer trainer for my 7 years old boy, I find it difficult to find and book extra training hours for our club and my sons team. Sometimes it's hard to find and book available training hours, and the contact information, and communication with venue owners are not easy obtainable. There is missing a suitable convenient tool to make this easier.

Tomas Lønsetteig

CEO of MyVenYoo

And then I asked someone else about their experience

It turned out that I'm not alone and there is no single convenient tool for scheduling, managing, monitoring, booking, and communication. Read below what the operating manager at Bedriftsidretten say:

Our goal is to facilitate an healthy community, and we are one of the most crucial provider of sports activities for adults. Our members need easy access to sport facilities and training hours for performing sports activities. I believe MyVenYoo will provide more training hours for our members, and contribute to a healthier community.

Lars Nystadbakk

Operating manager at Bedriftsidretten i Nordland

So, what have we created?

We've studied the existing market for finding, and booking available sports venues and training hours, and figured out how we can solve the existing problems. We decided to create a sky-based 24/7/365 tool helping you find available venues and training hours close to where you live, work and do sports activities.

Find

We provide a complex, but yet simple tool, to accurate let you search and find available training hours in your city. We make it easy for you to do a specific search based on location and activity.

Book

We share with you a booking tool making your bookings smooth and simple. No more hard-to-find available training hours, and no more poor communication with the facility owner. We make it simple for you to do the booking and communicate with facility owners.

Play

With an easy to pay as you go solution, we let you play in your favorite facility. We believe this makes your planning and training schedule simple and easy. In the end, it's all about having fun. Right? We help you on your way.
